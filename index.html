Why MIA Exists

The bridge between
care and coverage.

Up to $0 is set aside to pay a doula for every pregnancy. Most of it is never claimed. Not because the care wasn’t given, but because the paperwork falls apart before it reaches a payer. In Oklahoma alone, SoonerCare covers more than 0 births a year.

The Clarity Check

Payers support doula care, and they do pay. Somewhere along the way the trust broke. Today, MIA is how we rebuild it, so providers and payers close the gap together, with integrity.

For Doulas

Speak freely.
MIA does the rest.

After a visit, open MIA and just talk. About 90 seconds for a prenatal or postpartum visit, a little more for a birth. This is your chance to say what really happened, and what it took to be the support your client needed.

MIA checks your note against all of your state’s requirements and builds a clean, submission-ready record. Starting in Oklahoma, you get paid for the care you gave. MIA’s got you.

“I love this. The doulas I mentor could really benefit from something like this. Documentation is one of the top challenges we have with them.”
Jacquelyn Clemmons, Systems Change Advocate
Just Talk
Speak naturally after your visit. No templates, no checkboxes, no clinical words required. MIA understands how doulas talk about their work.
Checked As You Go
Every note is matched against Oklahoma’s SoonerCare rules. You see what is complete and what is missing before you close the app.
Private by Design
No client names needed to record. MIA handles your audio without personal details and adds identifiers only at the secure, HIPAA-safe export.
One-Tap Record
A submission-ready record in seconds. One tap and it is formatted, complete, and ready for billing.
For Payers & MCOs

Outcomes you can trust,
from the first visit.

MIA gives Medicaid plans and state agencies clean, checkable records from every community doula visit. The visibility and proof you need to grow doula coverage with confidence.

Drops into your system

Records arrive in the standard format your health data tools already read, so nothing gets retyped or cleaned up by hand. (FHIR-ready.)

Outcomes you can see

Anonymized results across the doula workforce. Visits completed, postpartum follow-through, in a form you can actually use.

Know your network

See who is active and how many visits they are doing, without manual audits or claim-by-claim digging.

Verified work before it reaches you

Every visit is checked against your state’s rules first.

Built by someone who was in that room.

Mia is a U.S. veteran, entrepreneur, and practicing birth doula serving families across Oklahoma. She built PDI because she lived the gap firsthand. Doing the work, getting the outcomes, and watching it disappear into the space between community care and the clinical record. MIA is the tool she needed and could not find.
